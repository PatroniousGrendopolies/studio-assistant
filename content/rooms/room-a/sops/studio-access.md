# Studio Access

How to get into the studio and how to leave it properly. This covers the physical access, alarm, and lockup procedure.

## Getting in

1. **Find the hallway lights** — the light switch is outside the studio door. Seriously, everyone misses this. Look for it on the wall in the hallway before you go in.
2. **Enter through the main door** — there's a keypad for entry. Check your onboarding document for the door code.
3. **Disarm the alarm** — the alarm panel is just inside the door. Check your onboarding document for the alarm code. If you don't disarm it in time, don't panic — just enter the code. If it goes off, contact Nigel.
4. **Grab the keys** — the lockbox is in the fridge/utility room. Check your onboarding document for the lockbox code. The keychain has the key for the mic locker (between the control room and live room) and anything else you'll need.

## While you're here

- The mic locker key stays on the lockbox keychain. Don't separate them — return the whole keychain to the lockbox when you're done.
- If you need to get into the computer closet for the Mac Mini, that's unlocked.

## Locking up

When your session is done:

1. **Shut down the studio** — follow the shutdown sequence in the power-on SOP (speakers off first, then computer, then power conditioner).
2. **Return all keys** to the lockbox in the utility room. Make sure it's locked.
3. **Arm the alarm** — check your onboarding document for the procedure.
4. **Lock the door** behind you.
5. **Turn off the hallway lights** — the switch outside the door.

## Important

All access codes, alarm codes, and passwords are in your personal onboarding document. They are not stored in this system for security reasons. If you've lost your onboarding document, contact Nigel directly.
